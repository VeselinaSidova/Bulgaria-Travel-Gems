Traveler

I travel alone in the night, in solitude —
and the stars shine for me from afar...
My heart is filled with the grace,
with which the night obstructs me.

Below me — both road and field, and seas —
and everything sleeps in the earth's darkness...
My heart is filled with the silence,
with which the night greets the traveler.

I travel alone in the night, in solitude...
And everything sleeps... And only I do not sleep —
my heart is filled with the great
love, with which the night walks the traveler...

Peyo Yavorov
